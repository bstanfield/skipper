About us

Our team of data scientists, designers, and engineers is on a mission to make work wonderful.



Most people will spend at least 80,000 hours of their life working. We hope that these hours will be fulfilling and even fun, but for most people their working hours cause stress and drain energy.


It doesn’t have to be this way—research shows that happier employees are more productive and bring more profit to their employers. At Skipper, we want to help every team build a better culture so they can be more productive as employees and happier as people.
