About us

Yeah, we each work hard to bring you a great product. But more importantly, we work hard together.



The three of us have worked together in teams (ranging from 3 to over 40 teammates) for the past two years, and noticed how culture changes when teams change and grow. With this in mind, we started Skipper as a simple research project. Our personal investment in the problem space grew as questions about diversity and inclusion in tech sprang up this year.

We’ve since developed Skipper into a full-fledged product that helps you tie people management decisions to your organization's bottom line. As a small, flexible team, we're excited to work closely with you to create a product that seamlessly integrates itself into your organization.

Our Philosophy on Inclusivity

We’re people first. We cherish the rich and intersectional narratives that our users bring to the table, and strive to create an inclusive product to suit their diverse needs. We understand the moral imperatives and bottom line arguments for more diverse and inclusive teams, and we believe that by helping these teams foster a wider range of perspectives, they’ll be able to deliver better products and services. The three of us bring a diversity of experiences to our work and strive to bring a diversity of backgrounds to our team as we grow.
